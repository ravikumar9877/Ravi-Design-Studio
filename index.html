faq
Questions &
Answers
What tools do you use? +
Figma for UI and prototyping, Framer for advanced interactions, Maze & Lookback for testing, Notion for documentation. Handoff via Zeplin or Figma's native tools.
What's your typical timeline? +
A sprint runs 4–8 weeks. A full end-to-end design (research → hi-fi → handoff) usually takes 10–16 weeks depending on scope and feedback cycles.
Do you work with developers? +
Absolutely. I provide thorough design specs, component libraries, and stay available throughout the development phase to answer questions and review implementations.
Are you available for freelance? +
Yes! I'm currently open to freelance projects and select collaborations. Drop me an email and let's discuss what you're building together.
